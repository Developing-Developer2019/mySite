My journey

My learning languages:

Hi, my name is Marvin Brereton.

I am learning front end , with prospects of learning to achieve Dev-Ops in the future.

I've been teaching myself through doing projects and taking part in courses.

I also currently live with 2 developers (one front-end, the other full-stack) who have broad knowledge in the industry and help further my progression if needed.

My previous career was working in hospitality as a chef, I did that for 10 years. I had a passion for cooking, but I had a dream to work with computers. I didn't follow that dream up until the start of 2019. But I believe that my life skill development in the kitchen can be transferred over to working in an office.

E.g. learning to be patient in a kitchen is key, I understand that working with programming and development, can create problems which with the help from patience can be solved. Challenge, after challenge.

With a cool head and a confident manner, anything, can be overcome.
